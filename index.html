About me

This be how I do

Software is a cool thing. On the one hand, I get to impress people by using big buzzwords like "responsive", "scalable", "scrum", and "platform". On the other, I get to see what that actually means, while building out large-scale projects and getting to interact with some of the smartest and most interesting people I'll ever meet.

I am particularly passionate about the video-gaming industry. Anyone who knows me well will have heard my speech about how I believe that video games are on the cusp of a "cultural revolution" of sorts, and is soon to become something so much bigger, and more relevant, than *just* an entertainment medium.

Life, Goals, and Sentimentalities

It is my dream to develop something, be it a game, an application, or whatever, that can make even one person see video games the way that I do.

That being said, the ultimate goal for me is to work on video gaming technologies that continue to push the industry forward. As technology continues to grow and we inch closer and closer to the creation of our future AI overlords, new ways continue to pop up for us to experience, participate, and enjoy in gaming, and I very much hope and plan to be a part of the next wave of game-changing (heh) technologies.

Currently, when I'm not helping prevent the next PSN crash, I'm exploring the MEAN stack in an effort to expand my JavaScript application skills, as well as dabbling in iOS development.
